Our story

Your Trusted Home Service Partner in Dallas

To provide Dallas homeowners with fast, reliable, and fairly priced home services they can count on.

Highland Park Service Pros started with a simple mission: bring honesty and professionalism back to home services. Founded by a team of experienced tradesmen, we saw too many homeowners get overcharged or left with shoddy work. We built our company on transparency, quality, and respect for your home.

Today, we serve thousands of families across Dallas. Our team of licensed plumbers, electricians, and HVAC technicians undergoes continuous training to stay ahead of industry standards. Whether it's a leaking pipe or a dead AC, we treat every home like our own.
