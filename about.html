Our story

Your Home’s Comfort Is Our Mission

To provide Dallas and Highland Park homeowners with honest, high-quality plumbing, electrical, AC, and duct cleaning services that exceed expectations.

Founded in 2009, Highland Park Service began as a small plumbing company with a big vision: to bring integrity and craftsmanship back to home services. Over the years, we expanded to include electrical, air conditioning, and duct cleaning, always with the same commitment to excellence. Our team has grown, but our values haven’t changed – we treat every home like our own.

Today, we’re proud to be the go-to provider for hundreds of families in the Dallas area. Our technicians undergo continuous training to stay ahead of the latest technologies and best practices. We believe in building long-term relationships, not just fixing problems. When you call us, you’re not just a job – you’re a neighbor.
