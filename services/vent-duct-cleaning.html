Vent Duct Cleaning

Improve indoor air quality with professional duct cleaning.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our comprehensive duct cleaning service removes dust, allergens, mold, and debris from your HVAC ductwork. This improves air quality, reduces allergens, and helps your system run more efficiently.

What's included

  • HEPA-filtered vacuum extraction
  • Reduces dust and allergens in your home
  • Improves HVAC efficiency – lowers energy bills

How it works

1

Inspection

We inspect your ductwork using cameras to assess the level of contamination.

2

Agitation & Extraction

We use specialized brushes and air whips to dislodge debris, then extract it with a powerful HEPA vacuum.

3

Sanitization (optional)

We can apply an EPA-approved sanitizer to eliminate bacteria and mold spores for a deeper clean.

Common questions

How often should I have my ducts cleaned?

Every 3-5 years, or more often if you have pets, allergies, or recent renovations.

Will duct cleaning reduce my energy bills?

Yes, clean ducts allow your HVAC system to operate more efficiently, potentially lowering your energy costs.
