Overview

Our duct cleaning service removes dust, allergens, and debris from your HVAC system. This improves air quality, reduces allergies, and can lower energy bills. We use HEPA-filtered equipment for a thorough clean.

What's included

  • Removes dust, mold, and allergens
  • Improves HVAC efficiency
  • Reduces allergy symptoms
  • Leaves your home fresher and cleaner

How it works

1

Inspection

We inspect your ductwork with cameras to assess cleanliness and identify any issues.

2

Agitation & Vacuum

We use rotary brushes and a powerful HEPA vacuum to dislodge and remove debris.

3

Sanitize & Seal

We apply an antimicrobial sanitizer and seal any leaks to improve efficiency.

Common questions

How often should I have my ducts cleaned?

Every 3-5 years is typical, but more often if you have pets, allergies, or recent renovations.

Does duct cleaning reduce energy bills?

Yes, clean ducts allow your HVAC system to run more efficiently, potentially lowering energy costs.
